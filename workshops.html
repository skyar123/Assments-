Flagship Programs

Two workshops. Real tools. Immediate impact.

These are the workshops schools and programs keep inviting us back for. Both are grounded in evidence, built for practice, and designed for the actual classroom - not a theoretical one.

Flagship Program

The BEAR Program Workshop

Every child gets a bear. Every teacher gets a framework. Every classroom gets a shared emotional language.

Developed at Bank Street College’s Center for Emotionally Responsive Practice, the BEAR Program gives children a personal teddy bear as a portable emotional anchor - something they own, name, and bring home. The workshop trains educators to integrate BEAR into circle time, transitions, conflict resolution, and daily routines.

Who it’s for Preschool & K–2 teachers, Head Start staff, early ed program directors, family childcare providers
What you leave with A BEAR kit, BEAR Journal materials, 6-month implementation checklist, and strategies you can use Monday morning
Half-Day Intro Full-Day Deep Dive Multi-Session Coaching Series

Autism: It Just Makes Sense!

A full-day paradigm shift - not a checklist. Walk away with Shoebox Tasks you built yourself, ready for your classroom or home.

This is the training teachers ask for after their first year supporting Autistic children and realizing compliance-based approaches aren’t working. We decode autistic experience through a strengths-based lens - sensory profiles, visual learning, executive function, and the emotional architecture underneath the behaviors - then build practical TEACCH Shoebox Tasks together by hand.

Who it’s for Teachers, paraprofessionals, family support workers, program directors, and parents supporting Autistic children ages 2–10
What you leave with 2–3 Shoebox Tasks you constructed during the workshop, plus the engineering principles to build more. Immediately usable.
Full-Day Cohort EN / ES Available 25 Participants Max

Custom Engagements

We come to you

Most of our work happens on-site - in your school, your program, your community center. We build engagements around your staff’s actual questions, not a generic curriculum.

School-Based PD

Half or full-day professional development sessions built around your school improvement goals. Topics include BEARS, autism-affirming practices, trauma-informed care, positive guidance, and reflective teaching. Available in English and Spanish.

Program Consulting

Multi-session engagements for early childhood programs embedding relational practice into classroom culture, staff supervision, and family engagement. We stay long enough to see it stick.

Coaching Series

Ongoing reflective coaching for individual teachers or leadership teams. One-on-one or small group. We build capacity over time - not a single event that fades by Friday.
