What Circle Members can access

When you join, you’ll receive a welcome email with your first resource pack. Additional guides are sent through our monthly newsletter — you choose what’s relevant to your work.

For Families

Autism Assessments 0–3, ADHD & Autism 3–5, Field Guide to Connection, Autism with Toileting, LGBTQIA+ Resources, Toddler Screen Time, and more.

For Educators & Coaches

BEARS Coach Facilitator Guide, Teddy Bears in Guided Play, Trauma vs. Drama Play, Simple Reflection for Teachers, COS One-on-One Companion Guide.

For Program Leaders

BEARS 3-Part Anchor, BEAR Moments Scenarios, BEAR Program Scope of Work, BEARS Implementation & Leadership Model.

Monthly Field Notes

Curated clinical thinking, new blog posts, upcoming training dates, and one new resource per month — delivered directly to your inbox.
