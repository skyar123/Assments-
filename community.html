The Circle

Community, at the speed of care

ConnectEd Circles is a small practice and a slower kind of community. No algorithms, no infinite scroll, no performance. Field Notes in your inbox, Circles in person and online, and real humans - ours and yours - on the other end.

What You Won't Find Here

This isn't social media

The most honest thing we can say about our community is what it isn't.

Email, not algorithms

Field Notes arrives when we have something to say - not when an algorithm decides you need to be re-engaged. If we’re quiet, it’s because we’re with kids and families, not a content calendar.

Slow enough to land

We publish at the pace of clinical reflection - which is to say, not often, but when it’s worth your attention. No hot takes. No content farms. You won’t miss anything by stepping away.

Real humans replying

Reply to an email and you reach a person on our team. Our circles meet in rooms and on Zoom, with facilitators who know your name. No DM bots. No mass-comment templates.

A Quiet Promise
