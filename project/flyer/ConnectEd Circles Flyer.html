Who we are

Slow, attuned support for the people raising small children.

A team of educators and reflective practitioners walking alongside families, teachers, and programs across Western North Carolina. We hold space for the everyday moments where children grow.

i.

We sit beside, not above.

Slow, attuned practice with educators, caregivers, and clinicians.

ii.

We name what's hard, and refuse to leave it there.

Trauma in the room is honored. Hope is the through-line.

iii.

We work in English and Spanish.

Coaching, workshops, and family support, for families with more than one home inside.

What we offer
·
Coaching & Reflective Supervision

One-on-one and group sessions for educators, directors, and family support workers. In English and Spanish.

·
Autism Workshops & Support

Training, consultation, and family support grounded in dignity, sensory awareness, and lived experience.

·
The BEAR Program

Guided play with teddy bears in early childhood classrooms, inviting feelings, stories, and healing.

·
Field Notes

Writing, training, and resources from our practice, for anyone tending small lives.
